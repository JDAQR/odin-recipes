Fries

Description

A bowl of golden and cryspi fries

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • potatoes

Steps

  1. First preheat the oil to 160 C
  2. wash and cut your potatos into large sticks
  3. deep the potatoes in the preheated oil
  4. once cooked and golden remove them from the oil and pla them over a paper towel
  5. Enjoy!
